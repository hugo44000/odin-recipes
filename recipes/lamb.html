Lamb chops

Description

These baked lamb shoulder chops are very tasty and an easy main meal to prepare. Try serving them with mashed potatoes, peas, and pumpkin.

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 3 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 cups dry bread crumbs
  • 12 (5.5 ounce) lamb chops

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients and preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Beat together eggs and Worcestershire sauce in a shallow bowl.
  3. Place bread crumbs in a second shallow bowl. Dip each lamb chop in egg mixture, then lightly dredge in bread crumbs to coat. Arrange lamb chops in a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes. Turn lamb chops over and continue cooking to desired doneness, about 20 more minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center of chops should read 130 degrees F (55 degrees C) for medium doneness.
